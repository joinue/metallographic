Specimen Preparation (Class 2 Procedures)

Precious metals (gold, silver, platinum)

Description:

Pure Gold, silver and platinum precious metals are soft and smear easily. They are also very expensive.

Preparation Challenge:

Smearing is a problem with these very soft metals and alloys.
