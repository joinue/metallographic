Metallographic Equipment

Complete precision equipment for every stage of sample preparation, from sectioning and mounting through grinding, polishing, and analysis.

1

Sectioning

Precision cutting equipment for clean, damage-free sample sectioning. Choose from precision wafering systems for delicate materials or abrasive cutters for faster throughput. Minimizes deformation and heat-affected zones for accurate analysis.

Abrasive Sectioning
MEGA cutters and saws
Precision Wafering
PICO precision saws
2

Mounting

Compression and castable mounting systems with a complete range of mounting materials. Protect delicate features and ensure proper edge retention for analysis. Provides excellent edge preservation and sample protection during preparation.

Compression Mounting
TERAPRESS mounting presses
Castable Mounting
Vacuum, pressure, UV systems
3

Grinding & Polishing

Progressive material removal with PENTA hand and belt grinders, plus NANO-FEMTO grinder-polishers. Remove sectioning damage and achieve flat surfaces with controlled scratch patterns. Precise control over material removal rates and surface finish quality.

Hand & Belt Grinding
PENTA grinders
Manual Systems
NANO grinder-polishers
Semi-Auto Systems
FEMTO polishing heads
Controlled Removal
ATTO systems
4

Vibratory Polishing

GIGA vibratory polishers provide gentle, uniform surface finishing for delicate specimens. Ideal for EBSD preparation and applications requiring minimal deformation. Eliminates directional scratches and produces mirror-like finishes essential for advanced characterization.

Vibratory Polishers
GIGA systems
5

Analysis Equipment

Hardness testers and microscopes for comprehensive material analysis. Digital displays and automated processes support both research and production applications. Enables quantitative hardness measurements and high-resolution microstructural examination.

Hardness Testing
Microhardness, Rockwell, Brinell
Microscopy
Metallurgical, stereo, image analysis
6

Lab Furniture

Durable, high-performance furniture designed to support precision work and safety. From ventilated fume hoods and storage cabinets to ergonomic workbenches and microscope stations. Creates organized, efficient workspaces that enhance productivity and safety.

Furniture Solutions
Workbenches, storage, fume hoods
