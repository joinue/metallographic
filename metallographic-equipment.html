Sectioning

Abrasive Sectioning - MEGA

MEGA abrasive sectioning systems are built for precise, low-deformation cutting in metallographic applications. They handle a wide range of materials, including hardened steels and non-ferrous alloys.

Integrated cooling and adjustable controls help reduce thermal and mechanical stress, supporting consistent results for both routine and advanced material prep.

For compatible blades, visit our Abrasive Blades shop.

Manual Cutters Automated Cutters

Precision Wafering - PICO

Precision wafering is essential for creating thin, uniform sections of materials for microstructural analysis, especially with brittle ceramics and advanced composites.

Our equipment provides consistent accuracy and surface quality while minimizing subsurface damage. Adjustable speed settings and precision fixtures offer control throughout the cutting process.

For compatible blades, visit our Precision Cutting Blades shop.

Precision Wafering Saws

Mounting

Compression Mounting - TERAPRESS

Compression mounting provides a solid, stable base for metallographic analysis and is essential for preparing samples with consistent geometry.

Our equipment supports a variety of resins and sample types, with temperature and pressure controls that help ensure proper curing and minimal deformation. Designed for ease of use, it fits seamlessly into both research and production workflows.

Explore our full range of related consumables here: Compression Mounting.

Castable Mounting Systems

Castable mounting is a flexible solution for embedding specimens in resin, providing support for handling, grinding, and polishing.

Our systems accommodate a wide range of sample sizes and shapes, offering strong adhesion and protection for delicate materials. Easy mixing and curing help streamline prep and maintain consistency across applications.

Browse our castable consumables in the Castable Mounting shop.

Grinding & Polishing

Hand & Belt Grinding - PENTA

Hand and belt grinding are essential steps in metallographic sample preparation, providing the initial surface finish needed for accurate analysis.

Our equipment is built for effective material removal and smooth, consistent results across a range of materials and sample sizes. Its rugged design ensures long-term reliability in demanding lab environments.

These tools are well-suited for producing flat surfaces that support precise microstructural evaluation.

Hand Grinder Belt Grinder

Grinding & Polishing Systems
NANO-FEMTO

NANO-FEMTO systems provide reliable grinding and polishing for a wide range of metallographic specimens, delivering high-quality surface finishes for analysis.

As a modular system it can support both manual and semi-automated operation, offering flexibility for different lab workflows and budgets. With consistent performance across various materials, they help streamline sample preparation and improve repeatability.

Browse our Grinding and Polishing shop for compatible consumables.

Manual Grinding/Polishing Semi-Auto Polishing Head Automated Abrasive Dispensing

Controlled Removal Polishing - ATTO

ATTO systems are designed for controlled material removal during polishing, helping achieve precise, defect-free surfaces in metallographic specimens.

With programmable settings and precision fixtures, these systems support consistent results across both routine and advanced applications. They allow fine adjustments to match specific sample and process requirements.

ATTO is well-suited for environments where accuracy, repeatability, and surface integrity are critical to analysis.

Controlled Removal Systems

Vibratory Polishing

Vibratory Polishing - GIGA

GIGA vibratory polishers are designed for gentle, uniform surface finishing, especially on delicate or soft metallographic specimens.

These systems reduce surface deformation while producing smooth, high-quality finishes with minimal operator involvement. Adjustable settings help optimize performance for different materials and applications.
