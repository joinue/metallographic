PICO Precision Wafering

PACE Technologies’ PICO wafering saws deliver precise, low-damage cuts for metallographic analysis. Models like the PICO-155S, 155P, 200, and 200A feature variable speed and digital feedback—ideal for delicate materials in aerospace, biomedical, and materials testing.

Features

  • Precision Cutting: All PICO machines feature variable speed control, providing accurate cuts with minimal microstructural damage to delicate materials.
  • Durable Construction: Built with robust materials and advanced mechanics, ensuring long-lasting performance and reliability under heavy usage.
  • Efficient Cooling Systems: Integrated coolant systems help manage heat, extending blade life and maintaining sample integrity during cutting operations.

Gravity Feed Precision Cutters

Gravity feed precision wafer cutting offers consistent and accurate slicing for delicate samples, ideal for small-batch production and research applications. This method utilizes a controlled downward force to ensure precise cuts with minimal operator intervention. Its straightforward design and lower operational complexity make it a cost-effective choice for laboratories needing high accuracy and repeatability. By reducing manual adjustments, gravity feed systems enhance consistency across various materials such as metals and ceramics, making them essential for meticulous sample preparation.

PICO-155S - Digital Controls

The PICO-155S precision wafer saw features advanced digital controls, making it ideal for high-accuracy laboratory applications. It is designed to handle precise cutting tasks with ease.

  • Control: 5-inch touchscreen with Digital LED display and control
  • Maximum Cut-Off Blade Diameter: 75 mm (~3 inches) to 175 mm (~7 inches)
  • Cutting Capacity:
    • Diameter: Up to 2 inches (50 mm)
  • Speed: 200-1500 rpm, continuously adjustable with LED speed display
  • Arbor Size: 0.5 inches (12.7 mm)

Equipped with a dual nozzle pump coolant system, the PICO-155S maintains optimal cutting conditions. Its 125W (24V) motor and micrometer feed provide high precision, with an accuracy of 2 microns and a cutting load capacity of up to 1500 grams.

Table Feed Precision Cutters

Table feed precision wafer cutting provides enhanced accuracy and consistency for a variety of materials, making it ideal for complex research and industrial applications. This method uses a controlled horizontal movement to ensure uniform cuts, reducing operator input and variability. Its advanced design and automation features offer increased efficiency, making it suitable for high-volume tasks that require repeatability and precision. Table feed systems are especially effective for cutting metals and ceramics, where maintaining structural integrity is critical.

PICO-200A Automatic Table Feed Saw

The PICO-200A is an advanced automatic cutting machine with versatile fixtures for precision cutting of irregularly shaped workpieces. Ideal for industrial and research applications, it handles a wide range of materials.

  • Maximum Cut-Off Diameter: 200 mm (~8 inches)
  • Cutting Capacity:
    • Diameter: Up to 2 inches (50 mm)
  • Speed: 500-3000 rpm, continuously adjustable with LED speed display
  • Arbor Size: 0.5 inches (12.7 mm)

Featuring four cutting modes - constant speed, pulse, automatic, and manual, the PICO-200A ensures precision across various materials. Its integrated laser alignment system allows for accurate sample positioning, enhancing cutting precision. The hidden cooling system supports both wet and dry cutting, while overload protection extends motor longevity.

PICO-200S - Manual Table Feed Saw

The PICO-200S is a precision wafer cutting saw designed for high-accuracy cuts, ideal for research and industrial applications. It offers efficient handling of various materials with precise cutting capabilities.

  • Maximum Cut-Off Blade Diameter: 200 mm (~8 inches)
  • Cutting Capacity:

  • Arbor Size: 0.5 inches (12.7 mm)

Designed for precision metallographic cutting, the PICO-200S features built-in blade immersion cooling, an electronic speed display, and dual left/right clamping vises to ensure accurate, stable, and efficient sectioning. Compact yet sturdy, this robust cutter delivers consistent performance in demanding lab environments.
