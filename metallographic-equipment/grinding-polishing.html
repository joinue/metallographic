Equipment / Grinding & Polishing

Metallographic Grinding & Polishing Equipment

PACE Technologies supplies grinding and polishing equipment for metallographic sample prep, from hand grinders to automated systems. Our machines ensure precise, efficient performance and high-quality results across a range of materials.

Overview:

  • Precision Grinding: Achieve flat, damage-free surfaces with robust belt grinders or the NANO grinder/polisher, ideal for initial material removal.
  • Progressive Polishing: Refine samples through sequential abrasive stages using manual or semi-automatic systems like the FEMTO, designed for consistent, repeatable results.
  • Controlled Removal Systems: Achieve ultra-precise material removal with the ATTO, a metered grinding-polishing system designed for fine control in advanced sample preparation.

Hand and Belt Grinders

PENTA Hand and Belt Grinders are critical tools for metallographic sample preparation, offering precise material removal and surface finishing. These machines are designed for durability and performance, ensuring consistent results in demanding applications across various industries, including aerospace and biomedical.

The PENTA-5000A is a versatile hand grinder equipped with silicon carbide rolls, ideal for precise grinding of metallographic samples. Its robust construction and efficient cooling system make it a reliable choice for consistent material removal.

  • Construction: Aluminum and stainless steel

Manual Grinder Polishers

The NANO series manual grinder polishers are essential for precise material removal and surface finishing in metallographic sample preparation. These machines, available in single and double wheel configurations, are engineered for consistent performance, making them ideal for demanding applications in industries such as aerospace and biomedical. The NANO series combines robust construction with advanced features, ensuring reliability and accuracy in every polishing task.

The NANO-1000S features a single 8 & 10-inch wheel, driven by a 1 hp dynamic torque servo motor. Ideal for precise manual polishing, this machine supports optional automation accessories for enhanced functionality.

  • Variable Speed: 100-1000 rpm

Auto Polishing Heads

The FEMTO series autopolishing heads deliver consistent, precise polishing for metallographic sample preparation. Compatible with NANO polishers, they feature individual and central force application, advanced torque servo motors, and variable speed control for exceptional accuracy. Ideal for aerospace, biomedical, and other demanding industries, FEMTO heads ensure efficiency and reliability in every polishing process.

FEMTO-1100S - Individual Force Autopolishing Head

The FEMTO-1100S is an individual specimen holder polishing head designed for precise, controlled polishing of 1-6 samples. Compatible with NANO-1000S and NANO-2000S polishers, this unit features a dynamic torque servo motor for consistent results.

  • Compatibility: NANO-1000S / NANO-2000S polishers
  • Motor: 0.54 hp (400 W) servo motor

Precision Controlled Removal Polishers

The ATTO precision-controlled removal polisher ensures micrometer-level accuracy in material removal. Ideal for plano-parallel sample preparation, it serves industries like aerospace, biomedical, and advanced electronics. With programmable settings, a 7-inch touchscreen, and dual micrometers for pitch and roll control, the ATTO delivers consistent, precise results. Designed for reliability and ease of use, it enhances sample preparation quality and efficiency.

Vibratory Polishers

The GIGA-S Vibratory Polisher is specifically designed for the gentle polishing of difficult materials, making it ideal for EBSD (electron backscatter diffraction) sample preparation. This polisher allows precise control of the polishing process by adjusting the voltage to change the rotational speed. The GIGA-S is engineered to enhance the efficiency of your polishing processes, providing consistent results across various challenging materials.

GIGA-S - Vibratory Polisher
