Equipment / Abrasive Sectioning

MEGA Series Abrasive Cutting

PACE Technologies’ abrasive sectioning equipment delivers clean, precise cuts for metals, ceramics, and more. Manual and automated models support efficient sample prep while preserving microstructural integrity.

Features

  • Optimized Cutting Efficiency: Available in manual or automated options, our machines feature variable speed control for optimized cutting across various materials.
  • Versatile Material Compatibility: Designed to handle a wide range of materials, including metals and ceramics, making them ideal for diverse laboratory applications.
  • Advanced Safety Features: All machines include emergency stop functions and safety lockouts to ensure a secure working environment.

Manual Automated

Manual Abrasive Cutters

Manual abrasive cutting provides precise control and flexibility, ideal for small batches and custom cuts. Operators can adjust feed rates and positioning, making it suitable for metals, ceramics, and composites. Its simplicity and lower costs make it a practical choice for highly customized applications.


MEGA-M250S Manual Abrasive Cutter

The MEGA-M250S is an economical manual 250mm wheel feed for hands-on control. It efficiently handles a variety of materials, providing versatile cutting capabilities.

  • Maximum Cut-Off Blade Diameter: 250 mm (~10 inches)
  • Cutting Capacity:
    • Diameter: Up to 2.5 inches (65 mm)
    • Height x Depth: 3 x 7 inches (75 x 175 mm)
  • Z-Axis Wheel Feed Range: 3.25 inches (82 mm)

The t-slotted stainless steel table, measuring 9.75 x 9 inches (250 x 230 mm), offers a stable platform for cutting. To maintain optimal performance, the MEGA-M250S includes a robust 14-gallon (53 liters) cooling unit. Its 3 hp (2.2 kW) asynchronous motor runs at variable speeds from 300 to 3000 rpm and is adaptable to cutting forces of 3-15 amps.

Automated Abrasive Cutters

Automated abrasive cutting enhances your overall precision and efficiency, ideal for high throughput labs. With programmable controls, our machines ensure consistent cuts across materials while minimizing operator error. Their advanced features make them perfect for applications demanding precision, speed, and scalability.

MEGA-T300A

The MEGA-T300A Automated Cutter is our only table-top automated abrasive cutting machine. It combines precision and automation for efficient cutting applications. It features both automated and manual table feeds with a manual wheel feed (300mm), offering flexibility and control in various operations.

  • Maximum Cut-Off Blade Diameter: 300 mm (~12 inches)
  • Cutting Capacity:
    • Diameter: Up to 4 inches (100 mm)
    • Height x Depth: 3 x 8 inches (75 x 200 mm)
  • Z-Axis Wheel Feed Range: 3.9 inches (100 mm)
  • Y-Axis Table Feed: 7 inches (175 mm) with a feed rate of 0.04-0.20 inches/sec (0.1 - 5 mm/sec)
  • X-Axis Table Feed: 1.8 inches (45 mm) with a feed rate of 0.04-0.40 inches/sec (0.1 - 10 mm/sec)
  • Pulse Mode: 0.5-5A

The t-slotted stainless steel table, measuring 11 x 18.25 inches (280 x 470 mm), provides a stable platform for accurate cuts. The MEGA-T300A's automated and manual capabilities ensure precise performance in demanding applications, making it ideal for high-precision tasks.
