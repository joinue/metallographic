Equipment / Precision Wafering

PICO-155S Precision Wafering Saw

The PICO-155S Precision Wafering Saw combines advanced digital controls with robust design, featuring a 5-inch touchscreen for intuitive operation. It offers a 2-inch cutting capacity and dual nozzle cooling to optimize cutting conditions. With a variable speed range of 200-1500 rpm displayed digitally, it provides flexibility for various materials. The micrometer feed accuracy of 2 microns ensures precision, making the PICO-155S ideal for labs focused on accuracy and efficiency in sectioning.

Features

  • Advanced Controls: 5-inch touchscreen with digital interface for easy operation
  • Sturdy Design: Ensures consistent performance in industrial settings
  • Optimal Cooling: Dual nozzle system for maintaining cutting conditions
  • Adjustable Speed: Continuously variable (200-1500 rpm) with digital display
  • High Precision: Micrometer feed accuracy of 2 microns for precision cutting
