Brinell / MacroVickers Hardness Testers

Brinell and MacroVickers Hardness Testers from PACE Technologies deliver precise, reliable hardness measurements for metallographic applications. Built to meet modern material testing demands, they provide accurate results across various test forces and sample sizes. Whether for routine quality control or advanced research, our testers ensure material integrity and compliance with industry standards.
