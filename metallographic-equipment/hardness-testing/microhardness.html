Microhardness Testers

Microhardness Testers

Microhardness Testers from PACE Technologies deliver precision and reliability for evaluating the hardness of thin films, coatings, and microstructures in metallographic applications. Engineered for accuracy and consistency, these testers offer versatile testing capabilities with Vickers and Knoop methods, making them ideal for both routine quality control and advanced research environments. With our range of microhardness testers, you can ensure detailed analysis and compliance with industry standards.

ALPHA-MHT-1000Z Digital Microhardness Tester

ALPHA-MHT-1000Z - Digital Microhardness Tester

Features

  • Test Force Range: 10gf to 1000gf for a variety of microhardness applications.
  • Precision Optics: Equipped with 10X and 40X objectives for accurate indent measurement.
