Manual Abrasive Sectioning - MEGA-T350S

MEGA-T350S Manual Abrasive Cutter

The MEGA-T350S is a reliable manual abrasive cutter built for precision in metallographic applications. Its manual controls enable meticulous accuracy. With a durable design, it ensures long-lasting performance in demanding environments.

Features Technical Specifications Related Consumables

Features

  • Manual Operation: Provides precise control over the cutting process.
  • Intuitive Screen Controls: Simplifies operation without unnecessary complexities.
  • Rugged Construction: Built for durability and long-term reliability.
  • Efficient Cooling System: Integrated cooling to manage heat and extend the life of cutting components.
